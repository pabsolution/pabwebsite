## 📋 What You Need to Do (Next Steps)

### 1. **Submit to Google Search Console** (CRITICAL)
This is the #1 way to get indexed and ranked:

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your domain: `pab-website.vercel.app` (or your custom domain)
4. Verify ownership (follow Google's instructions)
5. Submit your sitemap: `/sitemap.xml`
6. Monitor performance and fix any errors

**Why:** Google won't know about your site unless you tell them. Search Console is how you communicate with Google.

### 2. **Create a Sitemap** (DONE - but needs to be generated)
Run this command to generate sitemap:
```bash
npm install next-sitemap
```

Then add to your build process. For now, Google will crawl your pages manually.

### 3. **Build Backlinks** (High Impact)
Backlinks = votes for your website. More votes = higher ranking.

**How to get backlinks:**
- List your business on Google Business Profile
- Get mentioned on industry directories (SoftwareOne, G2, Capterra)
- Write guest posts on tech blogs
- Reach out to potential clients with your portfolio
- Share content on LinkedIn, Twitter

**Best backlinks:**
- Tech industry directories
- Software review sites
- Business directories
- Industry associations

### 4. **Optimize for Local + Global**
Since you're serving globally, optimize for both:

**Local:**
- Create Google Business Profile (free)
- Add your location and service areas
- Get customer reviews on Google

**Global:**
- Target keywords like "EPoS software worldwide"
- Create content for different regions
- Use hreflang tags (advanced)

### 5. **Content Marketing** (Ongoing)
Create content that ranks:

**Blog Post Ideas:**
- "Best EPoS Software for Restaurants in 2026"
- "How to Choose Booking Software for Your Business"
- "Custom Software Development Guide for Startups"
- "Cloud Solutions for Small Businesses"

**Why:** Blog posts attract organic traffic and build authority.

### 6. **Link Your Domain**
If you have a custom domain (e.g., pab.co.uk):
1. Update DNS records to point to Vercel
2. Add domain to Vercel project settings
3. Update Search Console to use your custom domain

### 7. **Monitor & Improve**
Use these free tools:
- **Google Search Console** - See how you rank for keywords
- **Google Analytics** - Track visitor behavior
- **Ubersuggest** (free version) - Find keywords to target
- **AnswerThePublic** - See what people search for

## ⚠️ Common SEO Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Keyword stuff (repeat keywords unnaturally)
- Buy backlinks (Google penalizes this)
- Copy content from competitors
- Use hidden text or cloaking
- Have broken links or 404 errors
- Ignore mobile users

✅ **Do:**
- Write for humans first, search engines second
- Build genuine backlinks
- Create unique, valuable content
- Keep pages fast and mobile-friendly
- Fix broken links regularly
- Update content regularly
